02  ·  Growth Through Value Creation  ·  Champions, C-Suite & Stories
Two-Tier Champion Model

A two-tier engagement model: free and unlimited builds the network — gated and exec-sponsored converts it to revenue.

On-Demand Topic Sessions
Free · unlimited · self-scheduled
  • Single-topic: feature adoption, use case, new team onboarding
  • Templated + repeatable — run by CSM or SE
  • Builds champion network bottoms-up; signals expansion readiness
↓  feeds  ↓
Strategic Workshop
Gated · multi-step · exec-sponsored
  • Requires discovery prep + aligned executive sponsor (budget owner)
  • Culminates in the Readback — ROI tied to exec priorities
  • Scarcity is the value — pre-renewal / pre-expansion only

Volume of on-demand sessions = qualification engine that points the workshop at the right accounts.

Appendix  ·  The Mentimeter Enterprise Value Model

One model. Four levers. Named inputs. Benchmarks stay constant (conservative, published). Account variables pulled per customer — same calculator runs against any enterprise account. Attribution shown as a range anchored at 20%.

The Formula
1 MEETING PRODUCTIVITY
Managers × sessions/wk × prep-hrs saved × loaded rate × 50 wks
Benchmark: 40% prep-time reduction = 45 min/session · $95/hr
2 TRAINING EFFECTIVENESS
L&D sessions/yr × retention uplift % × cost-per-retrain avoided
Benchmark: 60% knowledge-retention uplift, interactive vs passive
3 DECISION VELOCITY
Strategic decisions/yr × misalignment cost × % reduction
Benchmark: McKinsey — poor decisions cost Fortune 500 ~€225m/yr
4 RETENTION & ENGAGEMENT
Headcount × turnover % × engagement-driven reduction × replace cost
Benchmark: Gallup — 18% productivity / 21% profitability gap
